BearBites Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

BearBites ("the app") is a push notification service that alerts Brown University students when a dining hall menu item they have saved as a favorite is being served.

What We Collect

  • Anonymous device identifier. A random UUID is generated the first time you open the app and stored locally on your device. It is never linked to your name, Apple ID, email address, or any other identifying information.
  • Apple Push Notification token. When you grant notification permission, iOS provides a device token that lets us deliver push notifications to your device. This token is stored alongside your anonymous device identifier in our database.
  • Food favorites. The names of dining hall menu items you choose to favorite, and optionally the dining hall you associated them with, are stored in our database and linked to your anonymous device identifier.

What We Do Not Collect

  • Your name, email address, or Apple ID.
  • Your precise or approximate location.
  • Any device identifiers used for advertising or cross-app tracking (such as IDFA).
  • Browsing history, contacts, photos, or any data unrelated to dining favorites.

How We Use Your Data

The data collected is used exclusively to send you push notifications when a favorited menu item is served at a Brown University dining hall. We do not use your data for advertising, analytics sold to third parties, or any purpose unrelated to the core app function.

Data Storage

Your anonymous identifier, push token, and favorites are stored in a Supabase database hosted in the United States. Supabase is SOC 2 Type II certified. Dining menu data is sourced from the Brown University Dining Services API and is not stored beyond a rolling 14-day window.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains in the database as long as you use the app. To delete all data associated with your device, remove all favorites using the Favorites tab, which will leave only your anonymous device identifier and push token. To request full deletion of your record, contact us at the email below.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple Push Notification service (APNs) — delivers notifications to your device. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/legal/privacy.
  • Supabase — database and backend hosting. Governed by the Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Brown University Dining Services API — source of publicly available menu data. No user data is sent to this API.

Children's Privacy

BearBites does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is intended for Brown University students.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
